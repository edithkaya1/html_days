My favorite film is the following: "The Intouchables!

A French comedy

The Intouchables tells the true story of a wealthy, physically disabled risk taker, the picture of established French nobility,

who lost his wife in an accident and whose world is turned upside down when he hires a young, good-humored, black Muslim

ex-con as his caretaker. Their bond proves the power and omniscience that love and friendship can hold over all social and economic differences.
